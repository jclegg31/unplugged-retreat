Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get to the Unplugged retreat?

To get to the Unplugged Retreat at 333 Main St, Lake Geneva, WI 53147, you can either drive using a GPS or map service, entering the address for precise directions. If you prefer public transportation, check local bus or train routes to Lake Geneva, and then take a taxi or rideshare to the retreat. For personalized directions or any specific travel arrangements, feel free to call us at 800-355-9285.

What kind of team is right for the Unplugged retreat?

The Unplugged Retreat is perfect for any team looking to strengthen their connections and improve their collaboration. Whether you're a startup aiming to build a solid foundation, a corporate team seeking to enhance communication, or a creative group looking for fresh inspiration, our retreats are designed to meet your unique needs.

What size teams can you accommodate?

At Unplugged, we can accommodate teams of various sizes, typically ranging from small groups of about 5 members to larger teams of up to 50 participants. Our retreats are designed to be flexible, ensuring a personalized and impactful experience regardless of team size. If you have specific requirements or a team size in mind, feel free to reach out, and we'll tailor our offerings to suit your group perfectly.

Is there really no wifi available?

We truly mean it when we say there is no WiFi available during our retreats. Our goal is to provide a genuine opportunity for your team to disconnect from digital distractions and reconnect with each other and the natural environment. By eliminating WiFi, we create a space where meaningful interactions and deep focus can flourish, allowing your team to fully engage in the retreat activities and each other's company.
